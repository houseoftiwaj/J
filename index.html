Our Story

House of Tiwaj is a luxury fashion house with more than 10 years of experience, specializing in high class fashion designs and premium fabrics.

We offer bespoke and ready to wear pieces, sewing exclusively to our customers' measurements and preferences, no matter where they are in the world. Alongside our designs, we provide a refined selection of premium fabrics, carefully sourced to meet the highest standards of quality and elegance.

Blending contemporary style with timeless craftsmanship, every creation reflects precision, superior materials, and an uncompromising attention to detail.

At House of Tiwaj, luxury is not just worn, it is experienced.

Defined by taste. Perfected by time.
